What is Evil Guys???

Evil guys is a trading card game similar to Pokemon or Magic the Gathering. Your goal is to use your

Hench men to defeat your opponent by draining their health to 0. You also have Contracts, Deals, Gadgets and Lands to help.

I’m going to show you how to make an Evil Guys card today. To keep it simple, I am going to show you only how to make a henchmen card.

The Idea:

The first thing I do to come up with an Evil Guys card is come up with the idea of what it’s going to be.

I either think of some sort of creature or person I want to make a card on, or what gimmick/ability

the card is going to have. Sometimes I plan to make a card for a certain element and that influences what I make.

The stats:

Once I have a solid Idea on what the card is going to be I then must decide what stats

it is going to have. Firstly and most importantly, I decide what the cost to play the card is going to be.

In Evil Guys there are FIVE main elements, along with a couple other additional

elements. Tasty, Animalia, Goon, Elemental and Rotten are the main five. To pay for cards you

use engines of each element to generate energy.

Using the ability, other stats and what the card is, I decide

what elements and how much energy it should cost. Next is how much attack and health points the henchmen has.

This step sometimes comes after and sometimes comes before deciding the cost. Either way though

it should be kept in mind to balance the ATK/HP and the cost so I card isn’t too overpowered (a general rule : ex)

After that the final rule is to decide what rarity a card should be,

aka how often a card appears in booster packs. The general rule is that the

more complex and different a card is, the rarer it should be. I either think

of some sort of creature or person I want to make a card on, or what gimmick/ability the card is going to have.

Sometimes I plan to make a card for a certain element and that influences what I make.

The Art:

After all of the card has been concepted, it is then time to draw up the art.

Evil Guys art can really look however you’d like. I personally use a program called Krita and my sister uses Fire Alpaca.

To sumarize...

... what you need to do to make a henchmen card for Evil Guys is:

  1. Come up with an idea of what you want the card to be
  2. Come up with it's stats :
    • Health Points and Attack Power
    • Energy Cost

  3. Draw the art
